Google Analytics 4 ↔ ChatGPT Connector (n8n)

This internal tool lets authorised staff query aggregated GA4 metrics from ChatGPT via an n8n workflow that implements the MCP.

Privacy Policy Terms of Service

How it works

  1. You grant the app read-only access (analytics.readonly) to your GA4 property.
  2. ChatGPT passes your question to n8n through MCP.
  3. n8n fetches the relevant GA4 report, returns the aggregated numbers, then discards the data—nothing is stored.
